Getting Here

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is situated on a cliff overlooking a vast lake. Here’s how you can reach us:

By Train

The Hogwarts Express departs from Platform 9¾ at King's Cross Station in London. Be sure to run straight at the barrier between Platforms 9 and 10 to access the platform!

By Carriage

If you are a Muggle or wish to travel discreetly, magical carriages are available to transport you to the school. Keep an eye out for the Thestrals!

By Broomstick

Advanced students may choose to fly directly to Hogwarts. Ensure you have your broomstick license before attempting this method!

We look forward to welcoming you to our enchanting school! Safe travels, and may your journey be filled with magic.
