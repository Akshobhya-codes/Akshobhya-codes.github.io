Hogwarts Syllabus

At Hogwarts, we offer a wide range of magical subjects to ensure a comprehensive education:

Potions

Learn the art of potion-making, from simple brews to complex concoctions.

Defense Against the Dark Arts

Prepare for any dark forces that may arise with practical defense techniques.

Herbology

Study magical plants and their uses in potions, remedies, and spells.

Charms

Master the art of enchanting objects and casting spells with a flick of your wand.

Care of Magical Creatures

Interact with and learn to care for a variety of magical creatures.

With a magical curriculum designed to inspire and educate, your journey at Hogwarts will be nothing short of extraordinary!
