School Rules

At Hogwarts, we have certain rules to ensure the safety and well-being of all students.

1. Respect Each Other

All students must treat each other with respect and kindness.

2. No Weasley Products

Weasley products are now banned from Hogwarts.

3. Attend Classes Regularly

Attendance is mandatory for all classes.

Follow these rules, and you will have a magical experience at Hogwarts!

We invite you to adhere to these guidelines to maintain a safe and friendly atmosphere. Your cooperation is vital in making Hogwarts a place where everyone can learn and thrive.
