Hogwarts School Houses

At Hogwarts, students are sorted into one of four houses, each with its own unique characteristics and values:

Gryffindor

Known for bravery, courage, and chivalry. The house emblem is a lion, and its colors are scarlet and gold.

Hufflepuff

Valued for hard work, loyalty, and patience. The house emblem is a badger, and its colors are yellow and black.

Ravenclaw

Celebrated for intelligence, creativity, and wit. The house emblem is an eagle, and its colors are blue and silver.

Slytherin

Known for ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. The house emblem is a serpent, and its colors are green and silver.

Which house will you choose? Remember, the Sorting Hat knows best!
